Our team of legal professionals has extensive experience in investigating, procuring, and enforcing intellectual property rights, as well as in licensing and other business transactions in which intellectual property and technology are key components. Our Philosophy

Victory is always nice â€“ especially for us attorneys. However, our goal is to successfully defend our clients' interests and to protect their innovations in the best possible way, in particular to save costs and time. To this end we offer:

Full Service Consultancy

Besides legal issues, consideration of economic and technical aspects is also essential. In our consulting concept, we develop a comprehensive and individual course of action together with our clients.

Long-term Strategies

The timely staking of "claims" by strategically registering various technical, design and trademark property rights can help strengthen a client's competitive position in the long-term.

Competition Monitoring

On the basis of a systematic observation of the competition, a client's legal position can be specifically expanded, and collisions can be identified and avoided at an early stage.

Conflict Avoidance

Our primary goal is to prevent or resolve conflicts in the framework of negotiations with competitors. For this, we draw on our extensive expertise in communication, mediation and the formulation of contractual agreements.

If a dispute is reasonable or unavoidable in a particular case, we work together to develop a tailor-made strategy in order to successfully and efficiently carry out the necessary official procedures and legal processes - so that a victory won't come at a loss.
