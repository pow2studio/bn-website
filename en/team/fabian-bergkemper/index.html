Dr. Fabian Bergkemper

Dipl. Biol.

Dr. Fabian Bergkemper studied Biology at the Technical University Munich with particular focus on microbiology. He completed the masterâ€™s course there in the year 2013. During his thesis he focused on the functional analysis of native and recombinant bacterial enzymes for the production of bioenergy.

He subsequently received his doctorate at the Research Unit for Comparative Microbiome Analysis (COMI) at the Helmholtz Center Munich in the course of a research project founded by the German Research Foundation.
